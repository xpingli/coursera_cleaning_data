Activity: data collected based on 5 types of motions 1 WALKING 
2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
 3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
 4 SITTING
 5 STANDING
6 LAYING
.

Time: the signal is detected within the time domain.

Freqdomain: the signal is transformed into within the freqency domain.

Acc: the signal is detected from acceleration.

gyro: the signal is detected by gyroscope.

body: acceleration from the body movement.

gravity: acceleration from the gravity movement.

mean: the value is the mean value for each observation.

std: the value is the standard deviation for each observation.

xyz: signals on each axial-X, Y, Z.

jerk: jerk signal.

mag: the magnitude of x,y,z calculated based on euclidean algorithem.
